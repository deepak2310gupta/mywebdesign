    ABOUT US

    Started in 2014, ZeroKaata is a 100% vegan direct-to-consumer brand for women's lingerie, apparel, and jewellery. Being an Indian sustainable fashion brand, our entire product range across all the categories is manufactured by Indian Artisans without any cruelty.

    With sustained effort and a strong team, we’ve been able to create and maintain our position not only as one of the leading fashion brands in India but also as a Digital Organization that has touched the hearts of Millenials and Gen Z with its digital magazine (ZeroKaata Studio), social media networking, and Internship program.

    Founded by Gaurav Gambhir (ex Conde Nast-India, and Co-Founder-Ease Your Biz), our organization incorporates value-driven practices with an aim to create an unmatched, and unprecedented experience for everyone who was, is, or will be associated with our organization.
